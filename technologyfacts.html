8. Some people are afraid of technology.

People have all kinds of weird fears and phobias out there, and technology is no exception! 11. The QWERTY keyboard was originally designed to slow you down.

When typewriters were introduced, typing too fast would jam the keys. Using a QWERTY keyboard spaced out commonly used characters to slow typists down and prevent jamming. If you wanted to learn a more efficient keyboard, Dvorak is made for speed.
